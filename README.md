## 🧐 The Problem
Every time you take a photo, your phone leaks:
* **Exact GPS Coordinates:** Pinpointing your home or current location.
* **Camera Metadata:** Your device serial number and settings.
* **Timestamp Data:** Exactly when you were where you were.

Most "Cleaner" apps on the Play Store request **Internet Permissions** and **Contact Access** to harvest your data while "cleaning" it. 

## 🚀 The Solution: PixelPurge
PixelPurge is built on a **Zero-Cloud Architecture**. 
* **100% Offline:** The app does not even request Internet Permission. It physically cannot send your data anywhere.
* **Local Processing:** All analysis and "Ghost Protocol" scrubbing happens entirely on your device's CPU.
* **No Ads / No Tracking:** No creepy analytics or third-party SDKs.
